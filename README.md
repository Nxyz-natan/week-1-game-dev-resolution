# Five Days

> *A visual novel about nine months of almost something real*



## About

**Five Days** is a slice-of-life visual novel made with Ren'Py
You've sat next to Riley Park since September Nine months of passing worksheets back and forth Nine months of did you do the reading and can I borrow a pen
Nine months of almost saying something real
Now it's the last week of school You have five days left

## Story

The school year is ending and everything feels different People are signing shirts taking photos and saying goodbyes You keep finding yourself next to Riley  in clas in the library outside on the grass in a hallway that feels too small
Some things don't last forever Some things don't have to end
What you say and what you don't changes everything



## Characters

**You**  You've sat next to Riley since September Nine months of small talk and borrowed pens and almost saying something real

**Riley**  Draws little figures in the margins of their notes Keeps a sketchbook they don't usually show people Hard to read, but worth the effort

**Jun**  Organising the end of year party Appears from nowhere at the worst possible moments Knows exactly what's going on and will not stop mentioning it


## Endings

There are **4 endings** to discover depending on your choices:

 Good ending
 Bad ending
 Neutral ending
 Secret ending
